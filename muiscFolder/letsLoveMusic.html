History of Music

Music has very common in religious services and also can be find in the bible such as Psalms 150, Revelation 14:3-4 and Psalms 98:1-7. The styls of music has also changed to know what we know as pop, jazz, gospel, rock, and many others. Music is the only things that has changed with the times and has been good and bad in most cases. Music has also had instruments dating back to ancient times that have been long forgotten to instruments that are used in marching bands, concert bands, orchestras, and many others.
